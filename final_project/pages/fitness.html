How To Get Started!

Getting in shape or incorporating exercises may seem daunting at first, but we're here to let you know how to start today!




If you're new to the gym or haven't been in a while, don't think that you must stick to a strict routine lifting weights or running on the treadmill every day. Start small and do something fun! The key is to become active and stay active every day. Do what makes you feel good and make sure you're burning calories somehow. Here is a list of ideas to help you decide how to start:


  1. Put on some fun music and DANCE! Get your heartrate up by dancing around to whatever music you like, in whichever way you want.
  2. Go for a walk - early in the morning when it's a little cooler, or in the afternoon while the sun is going down and everything is golden. You'll feel golden too.
  3. Try Yoga! This is something you can do at home, at your own pace. It's a great way to get moving and feel amazing.

Top Tips


  1. Don't overdo it! A common mistake that many make, is working out too hard and too often. Remember to stretch, give your body time to rest, and find a workout plan that is sustainable.
  2. Everyone is new to this at some point. Don't be afraid to ask for guidance. Get a workout buddy! If you're working out alone, use YouTube as a resource to watch others perform exercises so you can learn.
  3. Take a group fitness class. They come in all forms so you can choose what interests you and the level of difficulty. You'll learn from others, break a sweat, and feel amazing!
