Grocery List


Living better means feeling better and feeling better starts by incorporating better foods into your diet. Here is a list of the best foods to shop for to get started:


  • Chicken
  • Salmon
  • Eggs
  • Almonds
  • Almond Milk
  • Oatmeal
  • Berries
  • Apples
  • Bananas
  • Almond Butter or Peanut Butter
  • Sweet Potato
  • Brown Rice/Quinoa
  • Spinach
  • Broccoli
  • Asparagus
  • Tomatoes
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Bell Peppers
  • Salsa

With this grocery list, you'll have all you need to feel great. These foods are packed with nutrients to will help fuel you in the best way. Read more to find easy recipes that you can try!
